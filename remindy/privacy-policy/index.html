1. Introduction

Remindy is a personal application focused on customizable lock screen widgets and related productivity or personalization features. This Privacy Policy describes the kinds of information that may be collected, how that information may be used, and the choices available to users.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on the features used, Remindy may collect or process:

  • Information you provide directly within the app.

  • Device-related information necessary for the app to function properly.
  • Widget configuration or personalization preferences saved by the user.

3. How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve Remindy.
  • Store your preferences and app settings.
  • Maintain app stability, security, and performance.
  • Develop future features and enhancements.

Some data may be stored locally on your device to support app functionality, personalization, and widget configuration. If future versions of Remindy include cloud-based features, this policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

5. Third-Party Services

Remindy may use third-party tools or infrastructure providers in the future for analytics, crash reporting, hosting, notifications, or similar operational needs. If such services are added, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

6. Data Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and security, or work with trusted service providers supporting app functionality.

7. Data Retention

Information may be retained only for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

8. Your Choices

Depending on the version of the app and your device settings, you may be able to manage permissions, app settings, notifications, and certain stored preferences directly from your device or within the app.

9. Children’s Privacy

Remindy is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to provide consent on their own. If it becomes known that information from a child has been collected improperly, reasonable steps may be taken to delete it.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational updates. The latest version will always be available on this page.
